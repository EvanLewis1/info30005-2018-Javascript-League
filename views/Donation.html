If your items are still in good condition, you could:
  • reuse them yourself
  • give them to friends
  • donate them to charity
  • give them away through a swap event or online
  • sell them.

The websites and organisations listed below will help you to give away your items for reuse and pick up second-hand items in lieu of new ones.

  • FreeCycling

Online: give away, swap, sell or buy

  • Gumtree
  • Quicksales
  • SwapAce

Donate computers, fridges

  • ComputerBank
  • PhoenixFridge

Other ways to sell your items

  • holding a garage sale
  • holding an online auction on Ebay, Trading Post, Gumtree or SwapAce
  • having a stall at a local market
  • posting your items for sale in community newspapers or noticeboards
  • placing a classified advertisement in the Trading Post or other newspapers.
